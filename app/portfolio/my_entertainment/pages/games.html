Dragon Age

This game is a role playing, single player game. In the game you can gain the use of other
characters, but can only have 3 others in your group at any time. It is fun and takes place during
a time where mages, monsters, warriors, and dragons exist.

Star Craft

This game is more of a building game. You build your base and barraks, as
well as train your soldiers. You can play humans, zerg, or protoss. The zerg will make you
think of starship troopers. It is a fun game to play when you have the time to play it.

World of Warcraft

This game is a MMO. It takes place during a time of magic and warriors. You have the alliance
versus the horde. It is a fun game that has so much to do. You can quest, raid, or do dungeons. You
can also do achievements and dailies. It has fishing, cooking, and archeology. There is something for everyone.
