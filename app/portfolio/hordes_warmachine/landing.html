Objective



Hordes and Warmachines is a Table Top Game, using figurine type pieces and cards detailing the pieces to play the game.$ This game is played on a high table that measures 4X4. Each game is played with a scenario in mind and terrain to add difficulty.$ The first play to complete the scenario or kill the other players Caster wins the game
